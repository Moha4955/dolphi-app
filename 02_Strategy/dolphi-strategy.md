# Dolphi — Product Strategy & Differentiation
*Australian All-of-Life Trusted Marketplace*

---

## Vision

> "Australia's home for everything. Buy, sell, and discover — safely."

Dolphi is Australia's first all-of-life trusted marketplace: a single destination combining the category depth of TradeMe, the trust architecture of Dubizzle, the social discovery of Facebook Marketplace, and the AI intelligence of next-generation platforms — purpose-built for the Australian market.

---

## The Core Problem We Solve

Australians currently face a broken marketplace landscape:

- **Facebook Marketplace**: Free but unsafe. 1 in 3 listings may be a scam. No buyer protection for local pickup. No verified identities.
- **Gumtree**: Familiar but declining. AUD 74.4M revenue, down 8% YoY. Just launched payments with NO buyer protection.
- **Fragmented verticals**: Carsales for cars, REA for property, Seek for jobs, Gumtree/Facebook for everything else. Australians manage 4+ accounts across 4+ apps.
- **No trusted C2C payments**: Default is still "cash on pickup" — inefficient, risky, limiting.

**The opportunity**: AUD 60B+ second-hand economy, growing 10%/yr. 35% of Australians already buy/sell pre-loved. The market is there. The trusted infrastructure is not.

## How Dolphi Wins

### 1. TRUST FIRST — The Dolphi Trust Layer
The single biggest gap across all competitors. Dolphi builds trust as core infrastructure, not a feature.

**DolphiVerify** (inspired by Dubizzle's verification + Australian digital ID)
- Link Australian Driver Licence, Passport, or myGov identity
- Face match / liveness check
- Earn **Verified Badge** (blue dolphin tick)
- Verified sellers get: boosted listing visibility, higher search ranking, Verified badge displayed on all listings
- Verified buyers get: access to Verified-only listings, Dolphi Pay buyer protection

**DolphiPay** (inspired by TradeMe Ping + Stripe Connect)
- Integrated in-app payment
- Buyer protection up to AUD 2,000 on all DolphiPay transactions
  - Covers: non-delivery, item not as described, significant defect
  - Resolution within 5 business days
- Split between bank transfer (ACH), card, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- BNPL via Afterpay or Zip
- Seller receives funds T+1 after buyer confirms receipt (or 7 days auto-release)
- Fee model: **Free for sellers** (casual). Small buyer service fee: AUD 0.99 / 1.99 / 4.99 by item value.

**DolphiScore** (inspired by TradeMe feedback + Dubizzle TruBroker)
- Persistent, public reputation score for all users
- Stars + written reviews, displayed on profile and listings
- Cannot be deleted; can be responded to
- Professional sellers (10+ transactions/month) earn "DolphiPro" badge

**AI Safety** (inspired by Dubizzle's 70 AI models)
- Malicious link/QR detection in all chat messages
- Scam pattern detection (price-too-good, advance fee fraud patterns)
- Auto-flag suspicious listings for human review
- Real-time chat sentiment analysis

### 3. AI-FIRST LISTING — Remove ALL Friction

Inspired by Dubizzle's "Sell with AI" (photo → full listing in seconds).

**DolphiSnap**
1. Open app → tap "Sell" → take photo (or upload)
2. AI instantly generates: Title, Description, Category, Condition Grade, Suggested Price Range
3. Seller reviews/edits → publishes in under 60 seconds
4. Price benchmarked against recent sold comps on Dolphi + scraped market data

**DolphiSpec** (for vehicles + electronics)
- Scan VIN barcode or licence plate → AI auto-populates make, model, year, specs
- Condition grade (1-5 stars) with structured checklist
- Service history upload
- Auto-generates standardised listing with all technical specs pre-filled

**DolphiEstimate** (for property)
- Enter address → AI generates property valuation range
- Sources: CoreLogic data, recent comparable sales, suburb trends
- Displayed on every property listing
- Agents can challenge/supplement with their own CMA

### 4. MARKETPLACE AGGREGATION — Don't Compete, Connect

Rather than purely competing with Facebook Marketplace, Gumtree, and eBay — **aggregate them**.

**DolphiSearch** (cross-platform search aggregator)
- Search on Dolphi surfaces results from:
  - Dolphi native listings (first)
  - Facebook Marketplace (via API/scraping — legal and common)
  - Gumtree (syndicated)
  - eBay AU (affiliate partnership)
- Dolphi-verified listings flagged prominently
- Non-Dolphi results clearly marked as "External — not covered by Dolphi Protect"
- When users click an external result → deep link out, but Dolphi captures the intent data
- Over time, users learn: "verified + protected = Dolphi only"

This is the TradeMe move: don't fight the competition for listings initially, make Dolphi the starting point for ALL marketplace discovery in Australia.

**Partner Listing Syndication**
- Sellers on Dolphi can one-click syndicate their listing to Facebook Marketplace and Gumtree simultaneously
- Dolphi becomes the single source of truth; other platforms become distribution channels
- All enquiries route back through DolphiChat regardless of where the listing was discovered

### 5. SOCIAL DISCOVERY — The Feed That Sells

Inspired by Facebook Marketplace's passive discovery + TikTok/Instagram UX patterns.

**DolphiFeed**
- Personalised discovery feed (not just a search interface)
- Based on: saved searches, browsing history, location, time of day, past purchases
- "Near you" radius filter (1km, 5km, 10km, 50km, nationwide)
- Stories format for premium/business sellers (60-second vertical video, auto-play)
- "Trending near you" — real-time popular items in the user's suburb/postcode
- Collections — curate items into wishlists, share with friends/family

**DolphiNeighbours**
- Suburb-specific community boards (beyond marketplace listings)
- Free items / give-aways
- Local service recommendations
- Event listings
- Lost & Found
- NOT a full social network — a purpose-built community layer on top of the marketplace

### 6. BUSINESS MODEL

#### Casual Sellers (C2C)
- Free to list (unlimited)
- Free to sell
- Buyer service fee: AUD 0.99 / 1.99 / 4.99 by item value (on DolphiPay transactions)
- Optional listing boosts: AUD 2.99 / 4.99 / 9.99 (featured, top of category, homepage)

#### Professional Sellers / Dealers (B2C)
- DolphiPro subscription: AUD 49/mo (up to 50 active listings) / AUD 149/mo (unlimited)
- Includes: Pro badge, analytics dashboard, bulk listing tools, priority support
- Car dealers: AUD 199/mo + AUD 9.99/vehicle/month
- Real estate agents: AUD 249/agent/month (includes DolphiEstimate, enquiry tracking)

#### Jobs / Services
- Free job seeker profiles
- Employer job post: AUD 99/30 days standard / AUD 249/30 days featured
- Tradesperson profiles: Free basic / AUD 29/mo verified Pro

#### Advertising
- Display advertising (CPM/CPC)
- Sponsored listing slots (auction-based, like Google Shopping)
- Local business spotlight (weekly/monthly local homepage feature)

#### Data Products (Phase 2+)
- DolphiEstimate API for mortgage brokers, conveyancers, insurers
- Market trend reports (suburb-level data) for real estate and auto industries
- Aggregated anonymised demand data for retailers and importers

## The Dolphi Brand

**Name**: Dolphi — playful, Australian, intelligent, trustworthy. Dolphins are social, smart, and distinctly associated with Australian coastal culture.

**Brand Pillars**:
1. **Safe** — every interaction backed by verification and protection
2. **Simple** — AI removes friction from listing and discovery
3. **Local** — built for Australia, neighbourhood by neighbourhood
4. **Honest** — transparent pricing, real reviews, verified identities

**Tone**: Warm, confident, straight-talking. Not corporate. Not startup-jargon. Australian.

**Design Language**:
- Clean, spacious layouts (trust through whitespace)
- Primary: Teal/ocean blue + warm sand tones
- Accent: Bright coral (CTAs)
- Mobile-first, native-feeling interactions
- Heavy use of photos (listings are visual-first)
- Accessibility: WCAG 2.1 AA compliant from day one
